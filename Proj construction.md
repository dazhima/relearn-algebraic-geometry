* Let $S$ be a graded ring. Let Proj$S$ be the set of all homogeneous prime ideals which do not contain all of $S_+$.

Proj$(S)$ is a scheme.
* The idea is to show that $D(f)_+$ is isomorphic to $Spec(S_{(f)})$ as locally ringed spaces. It is the same as saying the affine open set $U_0$ of $\mathbb{P}_k^n$ is isomorphic to $Spec k[x_{1/0},\dots,x_{n/0}]$.

Relative version of proj construction: Proj of a sheaf of graded algebras over a scheme $X$.
