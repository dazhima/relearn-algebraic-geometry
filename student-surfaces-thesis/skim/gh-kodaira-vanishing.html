Kodaira vanishing — the theorem the embedding reduces to

What this is

A vanishing theorem kills cohomology: it says $H^q = 0$ under a positivity hypothesis. Why anyone would want that: session 1 §4 built the exact frame — $H^1$ is the obstruction to patching local data into global sections. The embedding argument needs certain restriction maps to be surjective (sections that separate points and tangent vectors), each surjectivity sits in a long exact sequence, and the map is surjective as soon as the next $H^1$ in the sequence dies. Vanishing theorems are surjectivity machines.

Where it sits

Third step of the analytic route: consumes A2's positivity and the Hodge theorem's harmonic machinery; feeds directly into the embedding theorem's proof, and — on the horizon — upgrades to Nadel vanishing once singular metrics (A5) and multiplier ideals arrive.

The delta

You already proved a vanishing theorem, twice: session 1's $\deg D > 2g-2 \Rightarrow H^1(X,\mathcal O(D))=0$. Kodaira vanishing is its $n$-dimensional form, and the shape of the statement carries the same fingerprint: the canonical bundle appears in the statement, exactly like the $2g-2 = \deg K_X$ threshold on curves — and like the $\mathcal O(D)\otimes K_X^{-1}$ twist that §6 had to get right (the correction note in board-script: positivity is needed for $D - K_X$, not $D$ alone).

Curve case dictionary: "$L$ positive" $\leftrightarrow$ "$\deg L > 0$", and Kodaira vanishing for $K_X\otimes L$ $\leftrightarrow$ "$\deg(K+L) > 2g-2$, so $H^1=0$" — which is literally §5's four-line argument. The new content in dimension $n$ is that no degree bookkeeping is available; curvature does all the work (Bochner–Kodaira identity: positivity term beats the harmonic equation).

Key statement

Kodaira vanishing. $M$ compact Kähler, $L\to M$ positive. Then $$H^q(M,\ K_M\otimes L)=0\qquad\text{for all } q>0.$$ Equivalently (Serre-duality flip): $H^q(M,L^{-1})=0$ for $q < n$.
Honest gap flagged in the theorem note: for the embedding, the sheaves needing vanishing are ideal sheaves $\mathscr I_{xy}(L^k)$, $\mathscr I_x^2(L^k)$ — coherent, not locally free when $\dim M>1$. So bare Kodaira vanishing isn't enough as stated; G–H offers coherent-sheaf machinery (ch.5) or induction via hypersurface sections, and which route they run is exactly what hasn't been extracted yet.
