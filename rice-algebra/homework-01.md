## 1. Ideals
**(1)** Let A \neq 0 be a ring. Show that the set of prime ideals of A has a minimal element
with respect to inclusion.
**(2)** In a ring A, let Σ be the set of all ideals in which every element is a zero divisor.
Show that the set Σ has maximal elements, and that every maximal element of Σ is
a prime ideal. Hence the set of zero divisors in A is a union of prime ideals.

of them are straightforward verifications that you should do at least once in your mathemat-
